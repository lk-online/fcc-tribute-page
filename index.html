  • 1949 On December 4, 1949, Jeff Bridges comes to this world in a family of acclaimed actors and writers.
  • 1951 His (uncredited) debute at an age of almost 2 years old in the drama "The Company She Keeps".
  • 1958-1970 He and his brother make occassional appearances in their father's shows among other plays.
  • 1971 First major role came in the 1971 film "The Last Picture Show", for which he garnered a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor.
  • 1974 Nominated again for Best Supporting Actor for his performance opposite Clint Eastwood in the 1974 film "Thunderbolt and Lightfoot".
  • 1982 One of his better-known roles was in the 1982 science fiction film "Tron", in which he played Kevin Flynn, a video game programmer (a role he reprised in late 2010 with the sequel "Tron: Legacy").
  • 1993 The late film critic Pauline Kael wrote that he "may be the most natural and least self-conscious screen actor that has ever lived".
  • 1994 On July 11, 1994, Bridges receives a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame for his contributions to the motion picture industry. The star is located at 7065 Hollywood Boulevard.
  • 1998 Stars as what is arguably his most famous role, The Dude, in the Coen brothers' film "The Big Lebowski".
  • 2010 Best Actor at age 60 for "Crazy Heart". Bridges is one of the youngest actors to be nominated and one of the oldest actors ever to win an Academy Award.
  • 2016 Appeares in the film Hell or High Water, for which he received his seventh Academy Award nomination.

Bridges married Susan Geston in 1977. They have three children and a grandchild.

Bridges has studied Buddhism. He meditates for half an hour before beginning work on a film set. He has learned Transcendental Meditation.

Apart from being a very succesful actor. Jeff Bridges is also an author, an amateur photographer, a musician, a narrator, a philanthropist. In 1984, Bridges and other entertainment industry leaders founded the "End Hunger Network" aimed at encouraging, stimulating and supporting action to end childhood hunger. In November 2010, Bridges became spokesman for the "No Kid Hungry" campaign of the organization "Share our Strength". Bridges also supports environmental causes and organizations such as the "Amazon Conservation Team".
